About Us



Our names are Patricia & Willem, originally from The Netherlands but now living our dream in The Dominican Republic. We decided to come here to pursue a different life with more time for each other and our family. That the climate is a lot better here than in Holland is a big bonus, the most we enjoy here are the friendly Dominican people, the nature and the tranquility without all the big problems Europe (also The United States, Canada and Russia) is having at this time.

We speak Spanish, English and Dutch and we have associates that speak native Italian, French, German and Russian.

From advice about color changes to major makeovers.

Not only because of my coaching experiences, I really love working with people in combination with homes. Homes are emotion and I comprehend that.

It’s my proper goal to find the best home for you in the Dominican Republic!
